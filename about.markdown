I'm a climate finance practitioner with 15+ years across investment banking, asset management, and institutional investor strategy.

My work sits at the intersection of physical climate risk, capital markets, and the institutional plumbing that determines whether climate insights become actual allocation decisions. I believe the gap between climate science and capital movement is fundamentally a translation problem — and that's the problem I work on.

Currently I lead climate strategy implementation at IIGCC, a European institutional investor network representing 400+ members and €65tn in AUM. I architected the Climate Risk Integration Framework (CRIF), developed the first insurer-investor alignment programme, and lead our AI-as-enabler strategy for institutional climate integration.

Before IIGCC: Deputy Head of Natural Capital at CrossBoundary, Senior Climate Finance Consultant at Genesis Analytics (Senegal, Uganda, Egypt, South Africa), and structured finance at Bancolombia where I built a $2bn+ transaction track record in debt capital markets across Latin America.

**CFA Charterholder. MSc from LSE. Born in Medellín. Based in London.**
